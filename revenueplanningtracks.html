REVENUE PLANNING & FUNDING, LLC

 

INNOVATIVE SERVICES

Revenue Planning & Funding, LLC can provide the three (3) following types of Services to public and non profit park and recreation entities to enhance their large scale Acquisition Plans, Site Plans, Facility Plans, and Systemwide Master Plans that recommend costly Acquisition and Development projects.

  • Prepare in-depth Capital Improvements Revenue Plans that accompany and complement large scale Acquisition, Site, Facility, and Master Plans.
  • Pursue and obtain large scale Grant Funding Revenues through Block Grants, Categorical Grants and/or Legislative Grants to help fulfill the Acquisition Plans, Site Plans, Facility Plans, and Master Plans.
  • Mobilize pooled talent Revenue Personnel to help fund the Acquisition, Site, Facility, and Master Plans.

These essential Services can be provided to strengthen, fund and team an Acquisition Plan, Site Plan, Facility Plan, or a Master Plan through twenty (20) interrelated Tracks.
