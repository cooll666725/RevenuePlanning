CORPORATE FOUNDER

The Founder, President and Chief Executive Officer of Revenue Planning & Funding, LLC, Stan A. Hemphill, is one of our country’s leading advocates for Revenue Enhancements in the parks and recreation profession. He has incorporated this financial consulting firm to help fulfill the triple needs for (a) innovative Revenue Planning, (b) creative Grants Funding and (c) pooled talent Revenue Teams. This new firm has emerged as a result of his extensive Revenue involvements, accomplishments and research during the past five (5) plus decades. From his early years he has been an over achiever.
