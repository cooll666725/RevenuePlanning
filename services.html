REVENUE PLANNING & FUNDING, LLC

 

INNOVATIVE SERVICES

 

Revenue Planning & Funding, LLC can provide the three (3) following types of Services to public and non profit park and recreation entities to enhance their large scale Site Plans, Facility Plans and Systemwide Master Plans that recommend costly Acquisition and Development pro-jects.

 

  • Prepare in-depth Capital Improvements Revenue Plans that accompany and complement large scale Site, Facility and Master Plans.

  • Pursue and obtain large scale Grant Funding Revenues through Block Grants, Categorical Grants and/or Legislative Grants to help fulfill the Site Plans, Facility Plans and Master Plans.

  • Mobilize pooled talent Revenue Personnel to help fund the Site, Facility and Master Plans.

 

These essential Services can be provided to strengthen, fund and team a Site Plan, Facility Plan or a Master Plan through any of the ten (10) following Tracks.

 

Track 1  Services Activation

(Define, Contract and Initiate Services)

Services Scope, Services Schedule, Services Products, Services Compensation, Services Agree-ment, Client Documents, Potential Team Member Candidates

 

Track 2  Capital Improvements Overview

(Examine Capital Improvements Approach)

Capital Personnel, Capital Plans, Capital Projects, Capital Revenues, Capital Implementations

 

Track 3  Site, Facility Or Master Plan Coordination

(Coordinate With Site, Facility Or Master Plan Process)

Public Engagement Meetings, Fundable Elements, Capital Improvements Elements, Cost Projec-tion Components

 

Track 4  Professional Services Funding

(Pursue Funding For Contracted Services)

Revenue Planning Services, Revenue Funding Services, Revenue Personnel Services

 

Track 5  Client Revenue Assessments

(Identify General Perception Scores)

Revenue Involvements Assessment, Revenue Performance Assessment, Revenue Capabilities Assessment, Revenue Starting Positions Assessment

 

Track 6  Site, Facility Or Master Plan Review

(Document References, Applicabilities, Revisions, Ideas)

Revenue References, Reference Applicabilities, Reference Revisions, Reference Ideas, Cost Pro-jections/Timing/Basis, Revenue Information Assessment, Revenue Information Recommendations

 

Track 7  Team Members Orientation

(Introduce and Orient Members)

Team Members List, Meeting Agenda, Services Approach & Applicability, Planning Process, Reve-nue Plan Outline

 

Track 8  Revenue Plan

(Prepare Capital Improvements Revenue Plan)

Introductory Information, Revenue Plan Team Members, Revenue Plan Purpose, Goals & Object-ives, Key Questions To Be Answered, Golden Stairway Revenue Model, Implementation Revenue Shortage, Revenue Management System, Revenue Classifications/Types/Techniques, Revenue Ingredients Options, Revenue Relationships Options, Revenue Pursuits Options, Revenue Per-sonnel Ingredients Options, Revenue Personnel Positions Options, Revenue Plan Recommenda-tions, Revenue Plan Summary, Revenue Pursuits Details, Revenue Plan Documents

 

Track 9  Grants Funding

(Pursue Grant Revenues)

Project Candidates Research, Grant Program Candidates Research, Projects/Grants Scheduling, Project/Grant Authorizations, Project/Grant Applications, Project/Grant Presentations, Project/ Grant Agreements

 

Track 10  Revenue Personnel Activation

(Identify Resources & Personnel)

Personnel Services Authorization, Team Advertisements, Office Spaces, Resources, Position Pri-orities, Position Phases, Position Descriptions, Personnel Selections, Personnel Orientations
